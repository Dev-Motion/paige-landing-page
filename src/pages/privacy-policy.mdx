# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Paige, a Chrome extension that enhances your new tab experience. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to your privacy.

## Information We Collect

Paige does not store any information about or from you. Your settings are saved securely within your browser and never transmitted to us.

## Permissions

Paige requires certain permissions to provide its features. These permissions are used solely to enhance your browsing experience and are not used for any other purpose. The permissions include:

1. **History, Bookmarks, Tabs and Active Tabs:** These permissions are used to enhance the search functionality within your browser.
2. **Favicon:** Used to display the websiteâ€™s icon next to its link in your quick links or search results.
3. **TopSites:** Used to access your most visited sites and provide quick links to these sites.
4. **Notifications:** Used to send you reminders for your to-dos and daily targets.

## How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide the features of Paige and improve your browsing experience. We do not use this information for any other purpose.

## Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
